About Chemtrails

This site shows information about all of the air quality monitoring stations in Maryland. It also provides a map that details the PM-2.5 monitoring stations, their current readings, and guesses about the air quality based on those readings.

CO is carbon monoxide, a toxic gas that is more dense than air. It is reponsible for most fatal air poisonings in many countries. It can cause dizzyness, seizure, and even death.

SO2 is sulfur dioxide, a toxic gas that comes from volcanic activity and fossil fuel use. It can cause lung disease and death.

NO2 is nitrogen dioxide. It is a gas that can cause inflammation, create ozone, and worsen asthma.

OZ is ozone. It is a gas that can damage lungs and the nervous system and especally affects kids, teens, people 65+ people who spend time outdoors, and people who already have lung diseases.
